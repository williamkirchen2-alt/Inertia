A protocol for one stuck-point

Two opposite kinds of stuck need two opposite fixes.

Being unable to start and being unable to switch feel similar from the outside, but they run on opposite mechanics — so the same advice fixes one and makes the other worse. Sort which one this is, then choose the few moves that match.
